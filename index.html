Why HaiBot?

HaiBot is a new Discord bot that has come to Discord in style. It has many features, including a full suite of moderation commands. It may look small, but if you try HaiBot, you'll realise that it has much more to offer.

Please note that HaiBot is still in beta and may contain bugs. If you would like to report these bugs, please join the Support Server.

Features

Moderation
HaiBot comes with a full suite of Moderation commands. With strike commands coming in v1.0, it's going to get better!

Fun
Fun commands are included to make your server more active and friendly. Also, who doesn't like a laugh?

Utility
If you can't be bothered to open that app on your computer, never fear! HaiBot has a range of utility commands, including a google and IP command.
